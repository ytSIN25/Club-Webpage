FAQs

How do I become a CSSC member? ▼
Simply register for a free account on our website! Membership is open to all students enrolled in CS-related programs. There's no fee to join the club.
Are events open to non-CS students? ▼
Yes! While CSSC is primarily for CS students, most of our events welcome students from any faculty. Check individual event pages for specific eligibility requirements.
How can I join the committee? ▼
Committee positions are opened at the start of each academic year. Watch for announcements on our website and Discord. You can also reach out via this contact form to express your interest early!
Can companies sponsor CSSC events? ▼
Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with companies for event sponsorship, recruitment talks, and workshops. Contact us with the Partnership topic above and our Sponsorship Head will be in touch.
How do I suggest an event or workshop topic? ▼
We love hearing ideas from members! Use the contact form with the "Feedback" topic, or share your ideas in our Discord #suggestions channel. If we get enough interest, we'll organize it!
