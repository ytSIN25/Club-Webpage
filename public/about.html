Our Mission & Values

We believe every CS student deserves access to opportunities, community, and the tools to succeed.

🌱
Inclusive Growth

We welcome students at every skill level — from first-year beginners to final-year specialists. No gatekeeping, only learning.

🤝
Community First

We foster genuine connections — between students, alumni, and industry. Your network is your superpower.

🚀
Real-World Impact

We don't just learn theory. Every hackathon, project, and workshop is designed to build real, tangible skills.

💡
Innovation Mindset

We encourage curiosity, experimentation, and the courage to build something new — even if it fails the first time.

🔒
Integrity

We operate transparently, treat every member with respect, and hold ourselves accountable to the community we serve.

🌍
Diversity & Inclusion

We actively celebrate diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences — because great software is built by diverse teams.

// our journey

How We Got Here

2020
CSSC Founded 🎉

A group of 20 CS students started a weekly study group that quickly grew into a formal club. Our first event: a 12-hour mini hackathon.

2021
First Major Hackathon

Launched our annual 24-hour hackathon with 80 participants. Partnered with our first industry sponsor and awarded $1,000 in prizes.

2022
100 Members Milestone

Crossed 100 registered members. Launched our mentorship program connecting juniors with senior students and industry alumni.

2023
Industry Partnerships

Established formal partnerships with 3 tech companies for internship pipelines and tech talks. Launched our cybersecurity workshop series.

2024
500+ Members Strong

Became the largest CS club on campus. Hosted 40+ events, awarded $5,000 in hackathon prizes, and launched our alumni network portal.

2025
The Future is Bright ✨

Expanding our AI/ML workshop series, launching an open-source project incubator, and growing our industry partnership network to 10+ companies.
